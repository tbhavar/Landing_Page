Executive Summary

Established in July 2016 (Re-constituted in 2018), M/s. T R B & CO is a distinguished Chartered Accountancy firm providing a full spectrum of audit, tax, and financial advisory services. Headquartered in Nashik, we specialize in delivering high-integrity solutions to SMEs, corporate entities, and individual professionals across India.

With a legacy of nearly a decade, the firm is led by CA. Tanmay Rajendra Bhavar, bringing deep expertise in Statutory Audits, Project Financing, and Strategic Tax Planning. Our practice is built on the pillars of transparency, regulatory precision, and proactive business partnership.
